Listing to your need

1

The first thing we do at Creativo before starting a project is an in-person sit down to figure out what your needs, goals, and dreams. Because lets be honest, there is a possibly we may not the right fit for your business. And if Creativo cannot provide you the service we believe you need then we will make sure you find the right fit. At the end of the day it is our goal to help grow your business and this initial meeting will help determine if that is possible.

Project Discovery

2

Drawing on the information we will have gathered up to this point, it is now time to determine the look and feel of your site. We will factor in a multitude of variables such as target market and audience, scoop of location, device usability and various other criteria to help us create the perfect solution for your business. In this step of the process we will determine what your customer is not only looking for but what they want.

Storming our Brains

3

Good projects come from good ideas. You are experts of your respected business and customers the best and we are experts of the web. In this stage we will work together to layout the foundations of your site similar to a blueprint to your soon-to-be dream home. And through the use of wireframes and Photoshop mockups you will have the ability to know what your site will look like before it is created.

Getting There

4

Once we have the prototype set we will begin to create the actual site. From designing, adding content and pictures, to testing and verifying your site works properly. We make sure your website looks great on all major browsers, tablets, and phones so you have the peace of mind knowing your customers will have a great user experience regardless of how they access your site.

Delving the product

Here at Creativo we postulate the biggest difference between us and our competitors is once your site is live we do not consider our journey to be complete, but rather we believe our journey has just begun. We make sure your site is submitted to all the major data aggregators so search engines can find your location information, and new customers can find you. Furthermore, we employ state of the art analytic software which allows us to better know your users and their behaviors on your website to increase your traffic and conversion rate. But we do not stop there, we provide continual support and expertise whether you would like to make a simple text change or embark on an online advertising campaign you can count on us.
